skim the milk to make milk and liquid cream
(normal milk is 4% fat, we need 40% or more to make butter)
Half the cream input will yield butter

Mix/Whip milk to make whipped cream
If you continue to whip/mix it itll seperate into butter grains and butter milk
(everything must be kept cold!)
rinse/mix the butter grains in a cold ice bath(same amount of water as removed buttermilk)
makes churned butter
rinse more times to make 'pure' butter
kneed throughly to make it better, adding salt will further remove water
kneed in salt/seasonings to make salted/seasoned butter
